Interfaces

There are various interfaces you can enable on the screen to help you manage and playing your campaign. Those can be found at the top right of the screen. There is a small drop-down prompt which will bring down the menu for the various panels once clicked. Clicking the drop-down another time, will hide the menu again.

Player Interface

Has buttons to control the scenario as well as the player character.

Enemy Interface

Allows to easily control the enemies attack modifier deck.

Game Setup

You can load scenarios and monsters from here.

Options

Allows you to change some of the mod’s behavior.

Camera Controls

A panel which allows to switch to certain fixed camera positions.
